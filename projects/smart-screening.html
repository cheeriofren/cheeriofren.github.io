Project Overview

This innovative project leverages artificial intelligence to revolutionize the discovery of two-dimensional materials for photovoltaic applications. By analyzing multiple parameters including chemical composition, electrical properties, and optical characteristics, alongside sustainability metrics, we create a comprehensive framework for identifying promising materials for sustainable energy solutions.

Key Features

  • AI-driven material property prediction
  • Sustainability assessment framework
  • Interactive visualization of material properties
  • Real-time analysis of photovoltaic potential

Methodology

Our approach combines machine learning algorithms with physical models to evaluate:

  • Electronic band structure
  • Optical absorption
  • Material stability
  • Environmental impact
  • Economic viability
