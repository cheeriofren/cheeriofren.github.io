Innovation Overview

Introducing StressCare, an innovative platform designed for holistic stress management. By leveraging advanced IoT technology and personalized algorithms, StressCare continuously monitors physiological indicators (like heart rate variability and sleep patterns), behavioral patterns, and psychological factors. This comprehensive data enables the development of tailored strategies that adapt to individual needs, enhancing the effectiveness of stress management.

Recognition

Awarded a Gold Medal by the Indonesian Young Scientists Association (IYSA) at the Invention and Innovation Fair 2024, StressCare is dedicated to empowering individuals with the tools and insights for proactive stress management, fostering resilience, and improving overall mental well-being.
